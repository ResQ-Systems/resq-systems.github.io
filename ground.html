An intelligent, no-nonsense uplink. Ground Station is your portable relay.

Our system provides a complex datastream and it requires a bespoke, portable network. Ground Station is our solution, providing the networking support needed for a team to use Ranger to its full potential.

Portable Networking

Working in tandem with Connect, the Local Area Network provided by Ground Station provides an unparalleled connectivity between team members. With every connected member able to see what Ranger's operator sees, it is easier than ever to coordinate on the fly. WiFi 6 brings rapid connection speeds and high range, allowing distant users to access Ground Station's data without issue.

With the potential for each Ranger to talk to multiple Stations, Wide Area Networking allows for a breakthrough in ease of operations - unprecedented data sharing across a great distance.

Durable Design

Hardened against rainfall and rockfall, Ground Station goes anywhere your team can. Built to IP55 specifications, our water and dust-resistant device is designed to withstand demanding outdoor environments. Integrated strap points allow you to mount Ground Station to a backpack...

and forget about it.

Redundant Connectivity

Ensuring safety and minimising risk, Ground Station has redundancy measures built-in. A double radio link to Ranger allows for constant communication between networked ground and air systems. Mitigating software design integrates failsafes on both sides of the connection. A poor signal doesn't mean risking the drone or the mission.

Real-time Health Monitoring

Building upon the diagnostics built into the drone itself, Ground Station will constantly cross-check Ranger's data for any issues while monitoring its own uplink. Alerting you directly on Ground Station's LCD screen, or via Connect, it keeps your team in the loop. With Ground Station's monitoring, you'll never miss a problem.

Long Battery Life

An intelligent network is nothing without longevity. Ground Station will be powered by waterproofed, rechargeable battery packs, offering up to 10 hours of connectivity on a single charge. Designed to the same durability as the Station itself, and with USB-C support to allow quick and simple charging, each pack brings the power you need to keep your team connected.
