Ranger is the centre of our vision: an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle designed especially for its mission - to provide SAR and other industries a rugged, dependable and adaptable design, designed first and foremost to get people home, safely.

Ranger's main design philosophy is to give you options.

Currently in rapid development, Ranger is envisioned as a pentamotor, tandem-wing drone with VTOL, hover and cruise capabilities, featuring full autonomous control and an inbuilt compute module.

Working in tandem with Ground Station and Connect, Ranger will operate as autonomously or manually as required, without being needlessly complicated.

Completely autonomous flight from take-off to shut-down, or completely manual controls and observation. Adaptable to any mission.

Ranger is your system.

Specifications

A drone is only as capable as the quality of sensors it can carry; Ranger is no exception. Our suite delivers an optimised, effective balance of quality, weight, and price. Ranger's cameras provide your ground team with birds-eye clarity streamable* directly to their devices via Connect, with the choice of either a 35MP visual payload, or a visual / infrared pairing - granting maximum flexibility and low-light operability.

* Ranger's visual suite is expected to provide smooth HD video at 30fps directly to operator devices for manual observation and/or control. In autonomous modes, full quality images from both pure visual + V&IR suites will be intermittently streamed to operator devices, with snapshots available on-demand.
